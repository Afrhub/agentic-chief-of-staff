# Pricing & churn

We raised prices 20% in Q2. Watch [[churn]] closely — two enterprise accounts
already cited pricing. Our playbook when churn clusters with a price change:
pause paid acquisition, run retention outreach to at-risk accounts first, and
only revisit pricing after retention stabilises. Last time we did this, MRR
recovered within two weeks.
